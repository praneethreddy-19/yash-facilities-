Privacy Policy

At YASH FACILITIES, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our clients and staff. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard personal information in the context of our facility management and caregiving services.

Information We Collect
  • Personal identification (Name, address, contact details)
  • Medical history and health requirements for patient care
  • Financial information for billing and transaction processing
  • Employment history for career applicants

Data Protection & Cookies

We implement rigorous technical and organizational measures to protect your sensitive data. Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for functional performance and to improve your browsing experience. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Terms of Service

By accessing and hiring staff through Yash Facilities, you agree to comply with the terms and conditions outlined below. These terms regulate the service agreements between the staff, clients, and agency.

Hiring & Billing Agreements
  • Staff assignments are scheduled monthly or per service basis, as agreed in the contract.
  • Clients must treat all caregivers and domestic staff with dignity and respect.
  • Replacements are provided within 24-48 hours if requested with valid reason.
  • Direct employment of our staff without agency written consent is strictly prohibited.

Service Agreements & Termination

Either party may terminate the staffing agreement by providing a 15-day written notice. Any outstanding payments must be cleared before the release of the staff. We reserve the right to withdraw services immediately in case of staff safety concerns.
